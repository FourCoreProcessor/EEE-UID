Conclusion

1) We implemented two different approaches to optimize the yaw angle of a wind turbine: a Machine Learning (ANN-based) approach and a Reinforcement Learning (DDPG-based) approach.

2) The ANN-based model efficiently predicts the yaw angle using a dataset. However, it relies heavily on the quality of the dataset.

3) The RL-based model, trained in a simulated environment, learns optimal yaw adjustments dynamically.

4) The ML approach being preferable for real-time predictions and RL being advantageous for adaptive control in unpredictable environments.
